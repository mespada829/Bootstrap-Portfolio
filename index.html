About Me

I have worked in San Francisco New York and Puerto Rico, during my time in Silicon Valley I fell in love with the entrepreneurship community, and since then have dedicated my career to help entrepreneurs and startups grow. My passion lead meto create Tool Watcher, a startup that offers tailored advice and support to entrepreneurs and startup managers by helping them find the tools they need to lead their teams to success, and build a community with their users.
